The Route

Course & profile

Sagalla's climb runs the ridge road above Voi town, with a panoramic overlook of Tsavo East as the signature stretch. Route maps for this leg are finalised and reconfirmed with the county and local conservancies before each edition — check back closer to race day for the exact GPX file and road closures.
Tourism & Culture

What there is to see

Beyond the finish line, Sagalla has its own reasons to linger.

Sagalla Hills viewpoint

A steep rise straight out of the Voi plains with panoramic views over Tsavo National Park — the same hills that already host the community's annual Sagalla Hills Marathon.

WWI battlefield heritage

Sagalla was a strategic point in the East African campaign of the First World War; old battle sites and cemeteries nearby draw history-minded visitors.

Conservation

Why the terrain is protected

Every leg of the tour runs through land under active conservation — here's what that looks like locally.

Sagalla Ranch conservancy

A ~25,000-acre member conservancy of the Taita Taveta Wildlife Conservancies Association, part of the wildlife dispersal corridor around Tsavo.

Sagalla forest reserve

One of the gazetted hilltop forest patches of the Taita Hills — part of the Eastern Arc Mountains biodiversity hotspot.
