What We Are

A ride built on the terrain and the people of Taita Taveta

The inaugural mountain-biking tour in Taita Taveta County, run as six legs across the county's hilly regions on long weekends through 2026–2027 — for cycling enthusiasts who want breathtaking routes fused with the culture of the Taita and Taveta people, with rider safety as the non-negotiable.

Description

Rubber meets terrain across the hilly county, immersing riders in scenic, breathtaking views and the culture of the Taita Taveta people along the way.

Goal

An eco-friendly, professionally organised sporting event that showcases the region's cultural wealth in a safe, ecologically sustainable manner.

Value proposition

A professional cycling event working toward carbon neutrality, infused with local culture, with cyclist safety on and off the trail treated as central to a positive impression on the host community.

Theme

Cycling for the promotion of culture, environmental conservation, eco-tourism, and the safety of cyclists on Kenyan roads.

The Long Game

Building toward a permanent home for altitude training

Beyond the six legs, the Tour exists to help build a permanent high-altitude training centre spanning Taita Taveta's hills — open to multiple sports, with mountain biking as the founding focus. Every leg, every sponsor, every rider is part of getting there.

800m – 2500m

The elevation range the centre will span across Taita Taveta's hill ranges — enough vertical variety for genuine altitude conditioning, not a single fixed-height facility.

Multi-sport, MTB-first

Built to serve multiple sports long-term, but mountain biking is the founding discipline and the reason the Tour exists in its current form.

Funded by the Tour

Sponsorship, support, and partnership revenue from every leg feeds toward this — the Tour is the vehicle, the centre is the destination.

County-wide

Not tied to a single leg location — the centre draws on terrain across the region the six legs already cover.
